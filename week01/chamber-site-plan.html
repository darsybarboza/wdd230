St George Chamber of Commerce

As a hub for businesses, the St. George Area Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting our members, elevating the local economy and championing community prosperity.

  • 1. What resources are used for Chamber members?

    We offer constant training, digital and in-person networks to help connect companies with each other in addition to Advocacy The Chamber is committed to representing our member companies before government leaders.

  • 2. How can I become a member of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce?

    Becoming a member is easy! You can visit our website and fill out our online membership application, or you can contact our office for assistance. We offer different membership levels to accommodate various businesses and organizations.

  • 3. What are the benefits of Chamber membership?

    Chamber membership comes with a wide range of benefits, including networking opportunities, access to educational resources and workshops, marketing and advertising opportunities, and involvement in community events.
